# Hypotheses: ma_cross_example_v1

## Research Questions

1. Does a moving-average crossover signal predict next-day cross-sectional returns?
2. Is the long-short spread from sorting on the MA signal economically meaningful after costs?

## Expected Relationships

1. Positive MA crossover states (`signal=1`) should be associated with higher next-day returns.
2. Top-signal portfolios should outperform bottom-signal portfolios on average.

## Key Testable Predictions

1. Fama-MacBeth coefficient on `signal` is positive.
2. Portfolio long-short spread mean return is positive with significant t-stat.
